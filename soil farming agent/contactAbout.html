About Soil Farming Agent

Committed to sustainable agriculture through scientific soil management.

Our Story

Founded in 2010,Soil Farming Agent emerged from a shared passion for sustainable agriculture and soil conservation. Our founder, Dr. Reena Ghosh, a soil scientist with over 20 years of experience, recognized the growing need for specialized soil farming agents who could bridge the gap between modern agricultural practices and environmental preservation.

Over the years, we've grown from a small consultancy to a team of 15 dedicated soil specialists serving farmers, agricultural businesses, and government agencies across the region.

Dr. Reena Ghosh

Founder & Chief Soil Scientist
